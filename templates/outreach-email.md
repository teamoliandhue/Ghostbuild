# GhostBuild Outreach Templates

## Cold Email

**Subject lines (A/B test these):**
- "We rebuilt [Company]'s homepage — take a look"
- "[Their domain] — found something worth fixing"
- "A gift for [Company Name]"

**Body:**
> Hi [First Name],
>
> I came across [Company Name] while looking into [their industry] in [city].
>
> I noticed [one specific, real problem — e.g., "placeholder text live on your homepage" or "your Google Ads are landing on a page with 6 identical CTAs"]. That's costing you leads.
>
> So we rebuilt it. No charge, no pitch.
>
> Live preview: [Vercel URL]
>
> We also put together a 90-day growth plan and two ad creatives ready to run. Happy to walk you through it on a 15-minute call.
>
> Worth a look?
>
> — [Your name], Oli & Hue

---

## LinkedIn DM (under 300 chars)

> Hi [Name] — we rebuilt [Company]'s homepage after noticing [specific problem]. No charge.
> Preview: [Vercel link]
> Happy to share the full plan on a 15-min call.

---

## Follow-Up (Day 5 — if no response)

> Hi [Name], just following up on the preview I sent.
>
> One thing I didn't mention — we also wrote two Meta ad scripts for [Company Name] that we think could work well for your [September / next] intake. Would be happy to share.
>
> Still happy to do a quick 15-minute call if you want to see the full plan.

*Never send a third message.*

---

## Personalisation Checklist
Before sending, replace every instance of:
- [First Name] — use their actual first name
- [Company Name] — their brand, not legal entity name
- [specific problem] — pick the single most egregious issue from the audit, name it specifically
- [Vercel URL] — deploy the homepage first, get a clean URL
- [city] — their actual city
- [Your name] — Jizan or the team member sending
