## Meta Ad 1 — Bill shock (BLDC efficiency angle)

**Audience:** Indian homeowners 28-55, household income ₹10L+, currently shopping ceiling fans / appliances on Amazon, Flipkart.

**Format:** Single-image carousel (3 slides) or 15-second video.

**Visual brief:**
- Slide 1: Two electricity meters side by side. Left labelled "Standard ceiling fan". Right labelled "Havai BLDC". Both run for 8 hours. Right meter barely moves.
- Slide 2: Close-up of the Havai BLDC ceiling fan. Spec callouts: 28W, 5-blade aluminium, 2-yr warranty.
- Slide 3: Number reveal — "₹1,800 saved per fan per year. 4 fans = ₹7,200 saved annually. Fan pays for itself in 2 summers."

**Primary text:**
> Your fan is the most expensive thing in your house you're not measuring.
>
> A standard ceiling fan pulls 90-110W. The Havai BLDC pulls 28W. Run it 8 hours a day, every day, and the BLDC pays for itself in two summers.
>
> Same airflow. Half the electricity bill. Two-year on-site warranty across India.

**Headline (40 chars):** Half the fan bill. Same air movement.

**Description (25 chars):** BLDC ceiling fans from ₹3,499

**CTA:** Shop now

## Targeting + budget guidance

- **Geographic:** Tier 1 cities for awareness video ads, Tier 2/3 for performance ads (lower CPC, higher purchase intent for the value angle)
- **Seasonality:** 70% of annual ad budget goes Feb 15 - June 15 (pre-summer + peak). Throttle to 20% rest of year.
- **First-month budget recommendation:** ₹1.2L Meta + ₹80k Google. Goal: 50 first-purchase orders + 200 newsletter signups + 30 commercial WhatsApp enquiries.
