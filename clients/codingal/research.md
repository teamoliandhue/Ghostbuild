## What Codingal is
Online programming, coding & AI classes for kids (K-12). Live 1:1 and group lessons. Mission: "inspire school kids and teens to fall in love with AI & coding."

- Founders: Vivek Prakash (CEO, IIT Roorkee, ex-HackerEarth co-founder), Satyam Baranwal (COO, IIT Dhanbad, founded Skillovate)
- Backed by Y Combinator, Rebright Partners, GSF, Java Capital, Nurture Ventures, DayOne Syndicate, WeFunder, MS&AD
- Accredited by STEM.org. Partners with Google.
- Mascot: Cody the Penguin. Name = "coding" + "magical".
