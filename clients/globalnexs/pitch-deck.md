## Slide 2 — What We Found

**Headline:** Your website is costing you students every day.

**3 specific findings (with evidence):**

1. **Placeholder text is live in production**
   > *"Where did this incorrect belief that everyone dislikes praising happiness come from?"* — visible on your homepage today. Any student who scrolls past the fold sees this and leaves.

2. **6 "Contact Us" buttons, zero conversion funnel**
   You have high-intent visitors arriving from search — students actively looking for consultancies — and no mechanism to capture them. No free assessment offer. No urgency. No reason to act today vs. next week.

3. **You're paying for ads that land on a leaking bucket**
   If you're running Google Ads (your keywords suggest you are), every click you're paying for is going to a page that converts at roughly 1–2%. A conversion-optimised page should be at 6–10% for this category.

**Visual:** Screenshot callouts with red annotation circles on each problem.

## Slide 3 — The Redesign

**Headline:** This is what GlobalNexs could look like in 2 weeks.

**Visual:** Full-page scroll of the rebuilt homepage (link to live Vercel preview).

**Key changes we made:**
- Hero headline changed from "best consultancy in Kochi" → outcome-first: *"Land Your Seat at the Right University, in the Right Country."*
- CTA reduced from 6 generic "Contact Us" links → one primary action: *"Book Free Assessment →"*
- Added trust signals anchored to real numbers: 92% visa rate, 200+ placements, 20+ countries
- Removed placeholder text, reorganised services by student outcome, not feature list
- Built a 3-step "How It Works" section so prospects feel in control before they call
- Added FAQ that addresses the top 6 conversion blockers (fees, visa rejection, timelines)
