## Business Snapshot
GlobalNexs is a Kerala-based education consultancy helping students apply to universities abroad — primarily UK, Australia, Canada, and Europe. They handle end-to-end: admissions, test prep (IELTS/OET/PTE), visa processing, loans, and post-departure support. Partner universities: 100+. Years in operation: ~2. Team size: ~6 visible staff.

Their market is fiercely competitive in Kerala — dozens of consultancies fighting for the same student pool. Differentiation is almost zero across the category.

## Top 5 Problems

### 1. Placeholder text is live on the homepage
Under the country selection section, lorem-ipsum-adjacent content is visible in production:
> *"Where did this incorrect belief that everyone dislikes praising happiness come from?"*

This alone destroys credibility with any visitor who scrolls. It signals the site was never properly QA'd.

### 2. No conversion funnel — just "Contact Us" everywhere
The primary CTA appears 6+ times with identical copy. There's no funnel architecture — no lead magnet, no free assessment offer, no deadline-anchored CTA. Every page ends the same way. Users have no reason to act *now*.

### 3. Claims without proof
- "Best consultancy in Kochi" — no citation
- "75+ global admissions" — no timeframe, no breakdown
- "2+ years of experience" — this is actually a trust negative; it signals very new
- Team bios list titles only — no credentials, no education, no success stories

### 4. SEO is self-defeating
Headings repeat "study abroad consultant in kochi" and "abroad education consultants in kerala" 15+ times. This is 2015 keyword stuffing that modern Google penalises. There's no topic authority built through useful content — the blog exists but is a thin content farm.

### 5. Zero emotional narrative
Students studying abroad are making a life-changing decision. The site gives them a service list, not a story. There's no student journey, no case study, no counsellor personality. The emotional hook is "your dream of studying abroad" but it's never substantiated with a real person's real story.

## Opportunity Statement
If GlobalNexs had a site that matched the ambition of what they claim to offer — they could:
- Convert 2–3x more walk-in/search traffic without increasing ad spend
- Rank organically for high-intent queries like "IELTS coaching + visa Kochi"
- Build enough trust online that word-of-mouth referrals convert without a sales call

The gap between their current site and what a well-designed one could do is enormous. They're spending money (likely on Google Ads) to send traffic to a leaking bucket.

---

## Competitive Gap
Top competitors in Kerala study abroad space (IDP, Edwise, KC Overseas) all have:
- Real student photo testimonials with university names
- Country-specific landing pages optimised for search
- Free assessment / eligibility check forms as lead capture
- WhatsApp chatbots for instant response

GlobalNexs has none of these. They're behind the category standard, not just behind the leaders.
